- Bandwidth: 5 to 2000 MHz
- Patch length: 5 to 120 mm
- Patch width: 5 to 120 mm
- Patch height: 0.01 to 3 mm
- Substrate length: 10 to 200 mm
- Substrate width: 10 to 200 mm
- Substrate height: 0.1 to 10 mm
- Feed length: 1 to 100 mm
- Feed width: 0.1 to 20 mm
